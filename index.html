Our Story

Welsh Coast Pizza Co. was born from a love of great food, good waves, and the stunning Welsh coastline. We're a popup pizza business bringing authentic Neapolitan stonebaked pizza to beaches, surf spots, markets, and events right along the coast.

Every pizza starts with our slow-fermented dough, made fresh daily and cooked at blistering heat in our stone oven. We source the finest ingredients — San Marzano tomatoes, fior di latte mozzarella, and locally sourced toppings wherever we can — because proper pizza deserves proper ingredients.

From Pembrokeshire to the Gower, Anglesey to Cardigan Bay, you'll find us parked up wherever the swell is rolling in and the sunsets are firing. We believe great pizza should be enjoyed with sand between your toes, salt in the air, and your board drying off nearby.
